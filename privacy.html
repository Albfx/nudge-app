Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This policy explains what data Nudge collects, why, and what choices you have. We've tried to keep it plain.

1. What we collect

We collect: your email address (for sign-in); screenshots and notes you upload for analysis; basic usage data (such as how many analyses you've run); and payment status from our payment processor (we never see or store your full card details).

2. How we use it

We use your data to provide the service: to sign you in, to run AI analysis on the images you upload, to track your plan and free-usage limits, and to support you. We do not sell your personal data.

3. How AI processing works

When you request an analysis, the image you upload is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate feedback, then the result is returned to you. We use this only to fulfil your request.

4. Service providers

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run Nudge: Supabase (accounts and database), Stripe (payments), Vercel (hosting), and Anthropic (AI analysis). Each processes data only as needed to provide their part of the service.

5. How long we keep data

We keep your account data while your account is active. Uploaded images are processed to produce your analysis and are not used to train models. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these, email support@trynudge.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your information and to limit who can access it.

8. Cookies

Nudge uses only essential cookies and similar technologies needed to keep you signed in and run the service. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Children

Nudge is for adults 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used Nudge, contact us and we'll remove the data.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with a new date.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: support@trynudge.com
