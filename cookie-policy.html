Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Nudge uses cookies and similar technologies.

What we use

Nudge uses only essential cookies and local storage needed to run the service — primarily to keep you signed in securely between visits. We don't use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Essential cookies

  • Authentication — keeps your session active so you don't have to sign in on every page.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings, but signing in may not work properly without the essential ones.
