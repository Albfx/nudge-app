About Nudge

Nudge is an AI dating coach that helps you have better conversations. Upload a screenshot of a chat or your dating profile, and Nudge reads the dynamic and tells you what's working, what isn't, and what to say next.

What it does

  • Conversation coaching — share a screenshot of your chat and get honest feedback, three ways to reply, and a read on the other person's interest level and mood.
  • Profile rating — upload your dating profile and get a score plus concrete tips to improve your photos and bio.

The idea

Most people freeze up not knowing what to say, or send the same flat openers and wonder why conversations die. Nudge gives you a second pair of eyes in the moment — practical, specific, and judgment-free — so you can show up as a more confident version of yourself.

Nudge is a coaching aid, not a script. You decide what to send, and you're always responsible for treating the people you talk to with respect.
